Plain Quesadilla

Description

Quesadillas are a Mexican dish that consists of a torilla filled with cheese.

Ingredients

  • one tbsp butter
  • two flour tortillas
  • one cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a pan skillet large enough to hold one of the tortillas flat, melt Â½ of the butter.
  2. Fry one side of one of the tortillas, then remove it from the pan.
  3. Put the rest of the butter in the pan, then put the unfried tortilla in to cook.
  4. Sprinkle the cheese on top of the tortilla in the pan, then top with the previously fried tortilla, browned side up
  5. Press them together with a spatula and fry the quesadilla until the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove it from the pan and cut in wedges like a pizza.
